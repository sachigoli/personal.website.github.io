Book Cover Summary Rating
Red Queen
Victoria Aveyard		 In a future world where Silver Blood means you have powers and Red blood isn't special, a girl with Red Blood gets wrapped up in the world of Silvers 8/10 action, drama, twists, dystopian
Dragon Slippers
Jessica Day George		 Creel isn't your typical damsel-in-distress, for one, her guardians gave her to a dragon to see if a knight would save her. Instead, she bargained with the dragon for her freedom, and leaves her hometown to travel the world. 9/10 I read it in middle school but I love this book with all my heart.
Tess of the Road
Rachel Hartman		 Tess needs to get out of her hometown and through a painful adventure, finds what she needs. 7/10 From my recollection, a more realistic representation of what would happen if you went on an 'adventure'. Isn't all sunshine and rainbows.
Stardust
Neil Gaiman		 Tristran Thorn is not from this world. And he is a bit foolish too. His foolishness takes him on a journey into the faerie world. There are three parallel storylines written with beautiful narration. 9/10 The only missing point is because his name is Tristran and I can't say that. Also my favorite movie. The book and movie are very similar, and are written with the same intonation.
